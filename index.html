Measured Surveys for Property Licensing

Are You a Landlord
or Letting Agent?

If you need to license a property with the council — whether for an HMO, selective licensing, or mandatory licensing — you need accurate, professionally drawn floor plans. We make the whole process fast and straightforward.

Council licensing applications require floor plans showing room dimensions, usage, and fire safety information. Delays in getting these drawings can push back your licence — keeping your property off the rental market longer than necessary.

Snap Plan provides a fast, local, and flexible measured survey service across Newcastle, Gateshead, Sunderland, Durham, North Tyneside, and South Tyneside. We visit your property, take accurate measurements, and deliver council-ready drawings — typically within 48 hours of the site visit.

✓
On-site measurement — we visit and measure every room accurately
✓
Room dimensions in m² and ft² — clearly labelled throughout
✓
48-hour turnaround — from after the site visit, subject to property size
✓

✓

Get licensed faster — quicker drawings mean quicker applications and rental income sooner
